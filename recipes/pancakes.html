Pancakes

Description

This easy recipe makes pancakes that are light and fluffy. You only need a few simple ingredients that you probably have in your kitchen right now.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Melted Butter
  • One egg

Instructions

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together
  2. Melt butter and warm milk (we use the microwave)
  3. Whisk wet ingredients together
  4. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  5. Cook on a hot buttered skillet
  6. Enjoy!
